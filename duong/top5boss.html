1. Are Boss watches considered a quality brand?

Boss is known for primarily producing watches that are timeless accessories for most people. Since the early 80s, Boss has offered beautiful timepieces with a unique combination of precision and elegance. With various collections, the brand has ensured a watch fit for every occasion. The wristwatches, under the supervision of Boss experts, are made with utmost precision and luxury so that you can hand them down through generations to come.


2. How would you know if a Boss watch is a fake?

Boss watches are meticulously crafted and well-engineered. Any fake or replica watch will not be able to match the same precision or features such as fonts, bold indexes, beautiful sunray dials, etc. When buying a Boss watch, check for noticeable errors to distinguish, such as misspelt words, improper finishes, or peeling paint.


3. How to change a Boss watch battery?

Search for your nearest Helios watch store and take or send your watch to change the battery. Helios watch stores can also change the battery on your Boss watch. You can check out the Free Battery Replacement page on the Helios website. Never attempt to change the battery yourself. Your old battery will be disposed of/recycled properly.


4. Where is the model number on a Boss watch?

The serial number of the watch is displayed on the backside of the case. You can authenticate your Boss wristwatch by searching with the serial number online
