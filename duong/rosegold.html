FAQs

1. Which watch is in fashion, gold or rose gold?

For all those watch connoisseurs who like the exclusive and distinguish themselves from others, gold is a metal that combines class and distinction. Both Rose Gold and Yellow Gold watches are equally on the list of trending fashion all the time. Gold watches are all about luxury and fall under fashion statement pieces.


2. How do you clean a rose gold-plated watch?

Cleaning a rose gold-plated watch requires utmost care as it can cause unwanted scratches if not done properly. Take a small amount of toothpaste and spread it on a cleaning cloth, or you can also use a cotton bud. Apply the toothpaste over the area you want to clean on the watch and wipe them gently. Once done, you can start inside the strap and move outside to clean the watch band. Finally, clean the paste with a cotton ball or damp clean cloth to ensure no residue is left.


3. Can a man wear a rose gold watch?

Yes. Even though there is a general stereotype that pink rose gold watches are meant primarily for women only, many wristwatches also feature a rose gold dial adorned by men.


4. Does rose gold go with everything?

Rose gold is a perfect accessory that fits well with almost everything. Whether you are dressing up for an ethnic occasion such as a wedding or an evening party, Rose gold watches will always help you elevate your outfit.


5. Is rose gold Still in Style in 2022?

Fashion trends change all the time, however, rose gold watches have stood the test of time and still remain a hot favourite for most watch collectors. From helping accessorize formal wear to adding a classy touch to occasional attire, many brands keep refining and redefining their rose gold watch collections.
