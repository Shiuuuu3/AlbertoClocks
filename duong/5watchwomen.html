3. Is the Fossil Q Smartwatch water resistant?

Yes. The Fossil Q smartwatch boasts water resistance up to 30 meters, and the brand has also ensured a 10,000-stroke swim test, which means it can be worn while swimming in shallow water.


4. How do I set the time on my Fossil watch?

For Fossil smartwatches, refer to the below-mentioned steps to configure the time settings:
⋅ When the screen is dim, tap it to wake up the watch.
⋅ Swipe down from the top of the screen.
⋅ Tap on Settings. In case you are still looking for it right away, swipe left.
⋅ Tap on System Date & time. Choose your time settings.


5. Why is my Fossil watch showing the wrong time?

By monitoring the settings, you can self-configure the date and time for a Fossil smartwatch. However, a Fossil Hybrid Smartwatch will automatically update to show the same time zone as your phone once paired and will always read the time as your phone. It cannot be manually changed, but you must re-calibrate the watch if the time is incorrect.
